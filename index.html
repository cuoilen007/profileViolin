About Me

Ha Vy, born in 1996, is a person full of enthusiasm and passion, especially for music, with a particular love for the violin. From gentle melodies to complex classical pieces, the violin is an indispensable part of Ha Vy’s life. Not only a music lover, I'm always seeking to improve skills and explore new styles and genres of music. Every time the bow is picked up, it's a new journey filled with inspiration and challenges, leading me into boundless creative spaces

My Experience

Ha Vy, with 10 years of experience playing the violin since college, has spent a decade honing skills and exploring the depths of music. Starting from the early university years, the violin was not just a hobby but gradually became a burning passion
